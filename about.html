ISESC TECH COUNCIL

OUR AIM

This study circle work for the collaboration and coordination among different Science and Engineering branches to find new intuitive ideas and if required assist them to represent the university in national and multi-national technical events. It would be a platform for students to involve in activities which requires coordination from different branches e.g. Research work, projects, workshop and other training learning program.

IMPORTANT LINKS

Our Objectives

There can be various objective of Interdisciplinary Sustainable Energy (ISE) Study Circle which could be decided but basic and primary objectives should be:-

1. Providing platform for individual students to involve in various technical related activities

2. A dedicated forum where students could discuss about various projects and hence will be involved in catalyzing innovation and new ideas among students.

3. Assisting if required in supervising, regulating and propagating activities organized by individual department.

4. Coordinating and exploring other opportunities which would help students in enhancing their skills in various fields of science of technology.

5. Organizing industrial and educational tours for students.

6. Alumni and expert meet for students.

7. Providing platform for multi-disciplinary research work

8. Creating university image as one of the Premier scientific and technical environments around the globe.

Roles

There will be various roles carried forward by the ISE Energy Study Circle in order to execute different objectives of the study circle:

Technical forum It would be proper dedicated study circle where student would able to come and discuss about various innovative and technical ideas which would in turn help the students in progress of their skills. These forums would also work as a medium for catalyzing innovation among students.

1. Organizing and propagating different activities

The technical council would be involved in organizing and propagating different scientific and technical related events and activities been held at various schools. It would help the interested student to participate in these activities. These might include Workshops , Events , Quizzes , Common engineering aptitude test , Regular discussion on advancement of science and technology around the world.

2. Regulating and Supervising the activities

If required, the study circle would come forward to regulate and supervise the activities organized by different department.

Inter -Departmental coordination

Interdisciplinary Sustainable Energy Study Circle will be a platform for student to do or participate in activities and related work where they may require inter-departmental coordination. These may include two primary works such as

(i)Projects

There are various projects where students require help of multidisciplinary subjects .With a proper study circle they would be able to a create a proper team, which will help students to pursue their project in proper manner and excel at national and international level events.

(ii)Research work

Many students are involved in research work with proper dedicated council which could form a proper forum where they can collaborate with students of different branches to increase their level of research work.
